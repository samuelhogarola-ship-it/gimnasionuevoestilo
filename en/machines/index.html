02 · Before you visit

Quick questions.

For specific equipment or initial guidance, contact reception.

Are machines useful for beginners?
Yes. They can help you learn movement patterns and build confidence before combining them with other exercises.
Can I combine machines and free weights?
Yes. Many routines combine both depending on goals, level and training preferences.
