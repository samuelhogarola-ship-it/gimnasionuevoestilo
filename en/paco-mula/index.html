04 · Quick questions

Before starting.

The first assessment works best by speaking directly with the gym and explaining your goal, experience and dates.

Does Paco Mula prepare bodybuilders for competition?
Yes. This page is focused on high-level bodybuilding contest prep and related advanced processes.
Can I ask whether I am ready to compete?
Yes. The best next step is to call or visit the gym to assess your starting point, goal and calendar.
Can preparation include posing and final conditioning?
Preparation can cover training, condition, posing, presentation and final-stage decisions depending on each case.

Assess your preparation.

If you have competition in mind, call the gym and explain your goal. A serious conversation at the start is better than a poorly planned prep later.
