05 · Questions

Before you visit.

Quick answers before your first visit.

How do I join?
Call us or visit reception. The team will explain the current options and what you need to get started.
Is there a minimum contract?
Conditions depend on the option you choose. Please confirm them with reception before joining.
Can I see the gym before I join?
Yes. You can visit the facilities and ask questions. We recommend calling first so the team can make time for you.
Where can I check the latest opening hours?
General opening hours are Monday to Friday from 7:30 to 23:00, and weekends and public holidays from 8:30 to 14:00. For one-off changes, call +34 952 47 00 44.
Is parking available?
The gym is in central Fuengirola and does not guarantee parking spaces. Check nearby options while planning your route.
